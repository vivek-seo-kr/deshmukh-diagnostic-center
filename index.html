Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What documents are required for a test?

Please bring your doctor's prescription and a valid government-issued ID proof for registration.

Q. Is fasting required for blood tests?

For tests like Sugar or Lipid Profile, 10-12 hours of fasting is usually required. Please call us to confirm for specific tests.

Q. How long does it take to get reports?

Most routine reports are available within 4-6 hours. Specialized tests may take 24-48 hours.

Q. Do you offer home collection?

Yes, we offer home sample collection services across Sadarpur and surrounding Kangra areas.

Q. Are you open on Sundays?

We are closed on Sundays. Our regular working hours are Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
