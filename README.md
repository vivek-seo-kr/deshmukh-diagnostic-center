## About

This portfolio is designed to help patients find and connect with Deshmukh Diagnostic Center online. It highlights the center's diagnostic services and makes it easy for patients to book tests and get directions to the center in Sadarpur.

## Sections

- **Hero** — Introduction with center name and call-to-action
- **Services** — Diagnostic tests and imaging services offered
- **About** — Center's background and community trust
- **FAQ** — Common queries about tests and reports
- **Contact** — Address, phone, and location details
